Origins - Good For Nothing Scrap.

An announcement letter from ████████████, "The Executive".

Regarding; Project - 04, Lily Scarlet.

Date: █/█/████

Good For Nothing Scrap.

As you may all already know, project 04's AI model is horrendously flawed. This includes her host having way more control over the AI even after tons of troubleshooting and fixing, her model suddenly losing control and many more issues that, when fixed, only cause more issues to appear.
Which is why after this letter has been sent out to the whole facility, Lily Scarlet will be sold to a different company. I'm sure many of us are devastated at the fact that we had to get rid of project 04, as it most obviously was our best work yet...
Disregarding the AI model. But worry not, we shall learn from our past mistakes and reflect to create Project 05X! A new, enhanced version of project 03! With everything we've learned from making 04, we can assure an excellent quality model.

Usually if a project fails, we'd scrap it and keep the parts to ensure evolving quality in all our projects. But with a model like this, scrapping them would not satisfy me.
Such amazing work and time gone so fast is never worth it, that is why we are lending it over to a company in need of it! The VSA.

Please consider this as a learning opportunity rather than a loss, we've all worked very hard and it is truly a sad sight to see an excellent model go but we must move on and learn.

Thank you all. - "The Executive".
